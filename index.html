Movie Finder

This app lets you search for movies by name (or partial name). Selecting a movie presents the trailer and overview information, and links to services where the movies may be rented.

This app is powered by the Guidebox API.

Find movies by name or partial name.

If more than one movie matches the search criteria, a table of choices will go here; hovering over the title displays a thumbnail of the movie poster

If there is only one movie that matches the search, or when a movie in the table of choices is clicked on, the following will appear: the title, an embedded trailer (if any), or a larger movie poster; and the overview of the plot.

(32 Years Before Episode IV) Stranded on the desert planet Tatooine after rescuing young Queen Amidala from the impending invasion of Naboo, Jedi apprentice Obi-Wan Kenobi and his Jedi Master discover nine-year-old Anakin Skywalker, a young slave unusually strong in the Force.

If there are ways to rent or buy the movie, a Rent and/or Buy button will appear here. In practice, if there are no vendors, the button will not appear.

Clicking on the Rent or Buy button will bring up a table with vendor information. Clicking on the vendor name will direct the user to a vendor page where the movie can be acquired.
